Our Mission

To revolutionize fashion through sustainable practices and exceptional craftsmanship. We empower individuals to express their unique style while making conscious choices that benefit both people and planet. Every piece we create tells a story of ethical production, innovative design, and unwavering commitment to quality.

Our Vision

To become the global leader in conscious fashion, inspiring a new generation of style enthusiasts who value authenticity over trends. We envision a world where fashion serves as a force for positive change, fostering creativity, sustainability, and social responsibility across communities worldwide.

Project Overview

STYLEFORGED is a clean, responsive fashion website designed to showcase collections and brand content. It balances visual appeal with a user-friendly browsing experience.

The project began with team planning and UI design, which guided development using HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript. Animations were handled with CSS transitions and keyframes. The layout followed a desktop-first approach with responsive tweaks and was tested across major browsers.

Core features include a fading image carousel, modular product gallery, and responsive navigation. Custom CSS variables ensure consistent styling and easier maintenance.

Contributors include Tolulope Bukonla (Planning & Dev), Damilare Adekunle, Daniel Abiodun, Inimfon Isaiah, Joan Emanerame, Divine Ekeh, Maureen Okpara, among others. Tools: Figma, VS Code, Git.
